What We Do?


The Placement Cell of Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College strives to bridge the gap between employers and students ever since it was established. The Placement Cell ARSD College not only provides placement and internship opportunities but also provides students with industry specific knowledge to hone their skill set and transform them into productive and efficient beings. The Placement Cell regularly conducts seminars and workshops for developing various competencies among students and giving them an insight into the corporate world.
