What We Do?


The Motilal Nehru College Placement Cell has been dedicated to connecting employers with students since its inception. Beyond offering placement and internship opportunities, the MLNC College Placement Cell equips students with industry-specific knowledge to refine their skill sets, transforming them into productive and efficient professionals. It regularly organizes seminars and workshops to foster various competencies among students and provide them with insights into the corporate world. Through the efforts of the Placement Cell, students have gained access to prominent corporate entities like Deloitte, KPMG, EY, Wipro, and TresVista, among others.
